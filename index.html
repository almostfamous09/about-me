Biography

Jenna Gaudio currently serves as the VP of Product Management for the Inc. 500 Video Technology Company, Vydia, Gaudio drives the strategy behind agile product development and is responsible for optimizing communication and efficiency throughout the company. Known for her specialty in accelerating organizations in the growth stage, while cultivating an unmatched company culture, Gaudio previously built and executed a Marketing strategy for a NJ Video AdTech company that led to a $33 million acquisition in 2015. As an active community contributor, she serves as the Co-Executive Director of Jersey Shore Women in Tech , Advisor to the local thought-leadership non-profit, TEDxAsburyPark, and the Co-chair of Marketing for the TJ Martell Foundation Young Professional Committee.
